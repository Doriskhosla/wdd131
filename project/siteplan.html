Advanced Promotions SLC Site Plan

Advanced Promotions SLC Website Planning Document

Site Name

Advanced Promotions SLC

I chose this name because it clearly represents a custom apparel and promotional products company based in Salt Lake City. The name communicates professionalism, branding services, and product customization.

Optional domain availability: advancedpromotionsslc.com (example only)

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase Advanced Promotions SLC custom apparel and promotional product services. The site will highlight product categories, display examples of past work, explain the ordering process, and help potential clients understand how to request quotes or place custom orders.

Scenarios

  • What types of custom apparel and promotional items does Advanced Promotions SLC offer?
  • How can I place a custom order or request a quote for my business or event?

Color Schema

These are the selected colors for the website:

  • Navy Blue (#0A1A2F) Used for headers, navigation bar, and strong branding elements.
  • Bright Red (#D7263D) Used for buttons, highlights, and call to-action elements.
  • Silver Gray (#C5C6C7)Used for backgrounds, borders, and subtle accents.
  • White (#FFFFFF)Used for body text backgrounds and clean spacing.

Typography

The following fonts will be used throughout the website:

  • MontserratUsed for headings, titles, and navigation links.
  • Open SansUsed for body text, paragraphs, and general content.
